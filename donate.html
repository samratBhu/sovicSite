Return/Refund Policy:

The organization takes the responsibility to process donations as per the instructions given by our donors, online and offline. However, in case of an unlikely event of an erroneous donation or, if the donor would like to apply for a refund the organization will respond to the donor within 15 working days of receiving a valid request for the refund from the donor. The timely refund of the donation will depend upon the type of credit card/banking instrument used during the transaction. The donor will have to send a mail to the organization requesting the refund within 2 working days of making the donation, along with payment receipt and person details necessary for initiating the refund. SOVIC is not obliged to make refunds and may, in its discretion, decline any requests for refund of donations, particularly if a tax exemption certificate has been issued.
