OUR STORY

This initiative was started by a group of enthusiastic and socially committed students from Assam Engineering College Guwahati, hailing from diverse backgrounds with a common vision of social and community development. We aim to make a small but sustainable difference in society. AEC has always been a socially responsible institution and SOVIC is working as a Dag bearer to carry forward its humanely legacy.

OUR VISION

To work on inspiring youths to volunteer, instigating compassion into them and bestow them the tools to create transformative social change.



OUR MISSION

  • To work with an innovative and robust approach for the community upliftment of the disadvantaged and unprivileged.
  • To make people discover their potential for bringing about action and change.
  • To focus on youth involvement in community development and inculcate the sense of responsibility in them.
  • To create a global culture of engaged children and youth committed to a lifetime of humane service.
  • To intrigue the appropriate skills in youth necessary for catalysing sustainable development.
  • To empower the young minds to become agents of change in society.
  • To solve and eradicate social challenges.
  • To provide relief and mitigation on the occurrence of a disaster/accident.
  • To foster livelihood development and economic empowerment.
  • To create a greener and sustainable environment by promoting clean/renewable energy use.
