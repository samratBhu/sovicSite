ABOUT US

SOVIC is the social responsibility cell of Assam Engineering College, Guwahati. It's a youth-led and student body volunteering organization, working up to the society's consciousness regarding its betterment and general welfare. SOVIC stands for Social and Civic, which conveys our motto of bringing about social development, for the civic population. We are determined in bringing a positive social impact in our society and also motivate the youth to serve humanity selFessly. With its promising goals, it aims to touch more souls and share the lessons of humanity.

OUR FLAGSHIP PROGRAMS

PRAYAS

As a part of this initiative, we visit the orphanages and poor schools to interact with the students there.

DAAN UTSAV

Experience the joy in giving! This event has three phases; Vastra Daan, Bhojan Daan and Vidya daan.

FLOOD RELIEF

Many of our members along with their family have distributed essentials and shelter to the needy

CLEANLINESS DRIVE

Students are divided into groups and clean the campus. The waste products are recycled.

Over the last seven years, SOVIC has been working on inspiring youths to volunteer, instigating compassion into them and bestow them the tools to create transformative social change. This initiative was started by a group of an enthusiastic and socially committed group of students from Assam Engineering College Guwahati, hailing from diverse backgrounds with a common vision of social and community development.
